Hostel Finder

Hostel Finder: A Location-Based Recommendation System for Hostels and PGs with Transit Information

Objective

  • To gather ample, diverse, and representative data of high quality to develop precise and resilient machine learning models
  • To collect information on all the available paying guest rooms and hostels in the city
  • To retrieve paying guest rooms and hostels in a selected locality of the college or office, tailored to meet the specific requirements of the user
  • To provide information on various public transportation options available from the room to the office or college.
  • To suggest practical and economical options for hostels or paying guest rooms
